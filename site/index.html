Hello, I’m Aiyub

I am a prospective Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science with early and continuous research experience in Artificial Intelligence and medical data analysis. I currently work as a Research Assistant at the AI and Digital Health Technology Lab, University of Queensland, while also leading AI initiatives as a Senior AI Engineer at HawkEyes Digital Monitoring Limited.

My research focuses on medical image analysis, particularly extracting hidden patterns from complex, high-dimensional medical images to support personalized treatment, precision medicine, and transparent, trustworthy diagnostic systems.

My long-term goal is to advance impactful AI systems, especially where machine learning, biomarkers, and innovative deep learning methodologies can address complex real-world challenges and improve data-driven healthcare technologies.
